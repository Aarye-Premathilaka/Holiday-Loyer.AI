        Trust architecture

        Professional without pretending.

        HolidayLawyer.ai is an AI-assisted claims product—not a fictional law firm. The usefulness comes from the workflow, evidence and clear documents.

        Elara is always identified as software.

        The claimant is the sender

        The person with the claim reviews and approves the document.

        AI will not be allowed to invent compensation amounts or route coverage.

        The current demo stores cases only in the user’s browser.
