    Know the basics

    Your rights should not feel impossible to understand.

    Depending on the journey, passengers arriving three or more hours late may be able to claim standard compensation where the disruption was not caused by extraordinary circumstances.

    Swiss-specific caution European court decisions on compensation for long delays can guide Swiss authorities and courts, but are not automatically binding in Switzerland. The website therefore says “may qualify” rather than guaranteeing payment.
