Prototype privacy information

Privacy

This document describes the current static portfolio prototype. It must be updated before a public service, AI backend, analytics, email sending, user accounts or cloud storage are added.

Current data processing

The current prototype processes form information in the visitor’s browser. A case is stored only when the visitor presses “Save case locally,” using browser local storage on that device. The prototype does not transmit claim details to HolidayLawyer.ai, OpenAI or another backend.

Information entered by the user

The form can contain identity and contact information, flight and booking details, incident descriptions and evidence selections. Visitors should not enter unnecessary sensitive information.

Local storage

Saved case data remains in the browser until the visitor clears the case, clears browser data or removes it through browser settings. Downloaded case files remain under the visitor’s control.

Future AI integration

Before AI is enabled, this notice must identify the service operator, hosting provider, AI provider, purposes, retention periods, international transfers, security measures and the contact channel for access, correction and deletion requests.

Operator details required before launch

Add the real legal name, postal address, privacy email address and any required company or commercial registration details here before publishing the service as a business.

Official guidance

A production privacy statement should be checked against the requirements of the countries in which the service is offered. Swiss guidance is available from the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner.
