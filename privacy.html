Prototype privacy

Privacy

This notice describes the current browser-only prototype. It must be expanded before AI, accounts, analytics, email sending or cloud storage are added.

Current processing

Form data is processed in your browser. Nothing is sent to HolidayLawyer.ai or an AI provider in this version.

Local saving

A case is saved only when you choose “Save case on this device.” It remains in browser local storage until you reset the case or clear browser data.

Information entered

The form may contain contact, booking, flight and incident information. Do not enter unnecessary sensitive information.

Before public launch

The final policy must identify the real operator, contact details, providers, purposes, retention, international transfers and user rights.
