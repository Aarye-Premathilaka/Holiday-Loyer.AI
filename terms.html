Prototype terms

Terms of use

These are demonstration terms for the portfolio prototype. They are not a substitute for terms reviewed for the actual operator, business model and countries of launch.

Purpose

HolidayLawyer.ai organises user-provided travel-claim information and produces general assessments, checklists and draft correspondence. It is a document-assistance tool.

No legal representation

HolidayLawyer.ai and Elara are not a law firm, court, government authority or licensed legal representative. The service does not represent a user before an airline, authority or court.

User responsibility

Users must provide accurate information, review every generated statement, verify applicable rules and deadlines and decide whether to send a document. Users must not submit false, misleading or fabricated claims.

No guarantee

Assessments and estimated amounts are not guarantees. Eligibility can depend on jurisdiction, facts, evidence, contractual terms, extraordinary circumstances and decisions by airlines, authorities or courts.

Professional advice

Users should obtain qualified professional advice where a matter is complex, high value, time-sensitive, disputed in court or outside the supported workflow.

Operator details required before launch

Insert the operator’s full legal identity, contact information, applicable law, dispute process, pricing rules, liability terms and cancellation/refund terms before commercial launch.
